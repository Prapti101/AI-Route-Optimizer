ABOUT TSP
What is TSP?

The Travelling Salesman Problem asks: given N cities, what is the shortest route that visits each city exactly once and returns to the origin?

Why NP-Hard?

There are (N-1)!/2 possible routes. For just 20 cities, that's ~60 quadrillion combinations — brute force is computationally impossible.

Nearest Neighbor

A greedy heuristic that always goes to the closest unvisited node. Runs in O(N²) — fast and gives ~20–25% of optimal, perfect for real-time use.

Real-World Use

Used by Amazon, FedEx, Swiggy, Zomato and logistics giants daily to save millions in fuel costs and delivery time.
